RRR (2021)

**Genre:** Period action drama

**Directed by:** S. S. Rajamouli

**Main Cast:**

  • N. T. Rama Rao Jr.
  • Ram Charan
  • Alia Bhatt

Synopsis

RRR is a fictional story inspired by the lives of real-life Indian revolutionaries, Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju. The movie depicts their journey as they fight against the British Raj in pre-independence India.

Though not blood brothers, they become sworn companions, unaware of each other's true identities and motives. Their bond is tested when their paths collide in a high-stakes situation. The story explores themes of friendship, rebellion, and the fight for freedom.

Accolades

RRR received critical acclaim for its action sequences, performances, and direction. It achieved significant commercial success, breaking box office records in India.
