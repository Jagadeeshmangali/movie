Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)

Genre: Epic action drama

Directed by: S. S. Rajamouli

**Main Cast:**

  • Prabhas
  • Rana Daggubati
  • Anushka Shetty

Synopsis

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion picks up where the cliffhanger ending of Baahubali: The Beginning left off. It reveals the reason behind Kattappa, a loyal warrior, killing Amarendra Baahubali in the prequel.

The story follows Mahendra Baahubali, Amarendra's son, who grew up unaware of his royal lineage. After discovering the truth about his father's death and his mother's imprisonment by the villainous Bhallaladeva, Mahendra builds an army to fight for justice.

Accolades

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion became the highest-grossing Indian film at the time of its release.
