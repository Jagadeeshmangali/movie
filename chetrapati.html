Chatrapati (2005)

Genre:Action drama

Directed by: V. V. Vinayak

Main Cast:

  • Prabhas
  • Shriya Saran
  • Bhoomika Chawla

Synopsis

Chatrapati is a story about Sivaji (played by Prabhas), an orphan raised in a circus. He gets embroiled in a fight for justice when he stands up for a woman being harassed. This act leads him to uncover his royal lineage and his destiny as the rightful heir of the Chatrapati kingdom.

The movie explores themes of identity, duty, and fighting for what's right. It was a commercial success and helped establish Prabhas as a leading actor in Telugu cinema.
